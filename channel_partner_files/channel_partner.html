CHANNEL PARTNER ECOSYSTEM

At MJR Builders, we believe in associating with Channel Partners to scale up our business objectives & optimising the resources we already have. We encourage the engagement of Channel Partner Firms as well as Freelancers who are having a competent portfolio.

MJR HAS EMPOWERED CHANNEL PARTNERS IN THREE KEY AREAS

Technical Training (Lead Registration)

Customer Engagement

Price Management

Who is our Ideal Channel Partner?

As we focus on cultivating the right relationships, we follow a number of metrics to select our partners:

  • Are they equipped?
  • Do they have RERA Certification & the resources to invest in the partnership?
  • Are they enthusiastic?
  • Will the partnership help them reach their goals?
  • Are they efficient?
  • Is there a technical fit? A competence fit?
  • Do we have similar values?
  • Is there a culture match between both parties?
